Simple Baby Size

FRONTEND

React | Material-UI

Parents-to-be can use this website to calculate their due date. Based on that date, parents can click through each week and see how big their baby is when compared to a hand.

Users can select between hand sizes for a more acurate comparison. Due date can be based off of last missed period, date of conception, or a known due date.
